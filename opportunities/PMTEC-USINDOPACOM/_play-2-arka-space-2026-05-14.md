# Play 2 — ARKA-led Space Integration / SDA PWSA Pull-Through

## What the customer wants (PMTEC priority gap #7)

> *Andy Emslie (PMTEC Space Integrator) and Daniel Hannah (Director, SDA Pacific) detailed integration of EW and missile-warning capabilities and named SDA's Proliferated Warfighter Space Architecture (PWSA) as a key component with industry-collaboration opportunities.*
> — [s.2026-05-12-usindopacom-seeks-industry-par]

PMTEC wants industry to bring space-domain capabilities that **plug into the training-and-experimentation ecosystem** — sensor feeds that can stimulate exercises, threat data that can populate digital twins, ground-software pipelines that joint forces can rehearse against.

## What ARKA actually brings (per CACI press release + 10-Q)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Product line** | Electro-optical/infrared (EO/IR) + hyperspectral imaging space-based sensors |
| **Software** | "Agentic AI-based" autonomous threat-classification (CACI's term, verbatim) |
| **Mission framing** | "Robust geospatial intelligence for critical national security missions" |
| **Pre-acquisition customers** | National-security space customer base; specific names TBC via ARKA transition lead |
| **Track record** | Per CACI press release: "decades-long track record of superior performance" |
| **Acquisition financials** | $2,642.7M net of cash acquired; closed 9 Mar 2026; ~1,100 ARKA employees joined CACI; expected ~$150M FY26 revenue contribution |
| **What ARKA does NOT do** | **SIGINT.** That's CACI legacy (Spectral, Trojan, Pit Viper). Do NOT combine the two in pitch materials — red-team finding #3 caught this overreach. |

## The play

**Headline:** Pitch ARKA's already-commercial EO/IR + hyperspectral data + autonomous threat-classification into **SDA's PWSA pull-through demand** — using **DIU Hybrid Space Architecture (HSA) demos** as the first procurement vehicle.

**Three principles that constrain the pitch:**

1. **No integration claims for 6–12 months.** Pitch ARKA as ARKA — pre-acquisition customers, pre-acquisition product line. Don't claim a "fully integrated CACI + ARKA offering." That claim isn't true yet and competitors will exploit it.
2. **Stay in ARKA's actually-proven lane** (EO/IR + hyperspectral). Don't bridge to SIGINT — that's a separate CACI capability stack with a separate pitch and a separate set of customers.
3. **ARKA transition lead signs off on every pitch deck** before external use (Decision G discipline).

## Vehicle / pathway

```
DIU Hybrid Space Architecture (HSA) demo
        ↓
   Multiple demos underway: INDOPACOM, EUCOM, CENTCOM, SOUTHCOM
        ↓
   ARKA's commercial data + autonomous classification feed PMTEC training environments
        ↓
   Concurrent: SDA PWSA Tranche 2/3 commercial-data ingestion contracts
        ↓
   Long-tail revenue $50M–$100M per pull-through opportunity per PWSA tranche
```

## Constraints to enforce (red-team-grounded)

| Don't say | Why |
|---|---|
| "ARKA + CACI fully integrated" | False until integration milestone (Q3 2026). Competitors will ghost us. |
| "ARKA's SIGINT capabilities" | ARKA doesn't market SIGINT. Mixing legacy CACI SIGINT with ARKA risks loss of credibility. |
| "Agentic AI" by itself (no operational specifics) | Customer flagged as M&A marketing. Always translate: "autonomous threat-classification models over EO/IR + hyperspectral data, with model outputs (detection / classification / confidence) consumable as digital-twin inputs." |

## Risks + mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Competitors ghost us as "unintegrated company pitching disjointed data feed" | Lead with ARKA legacy customer references and operational continuity, NOT integration story. Make "no change for the customer" the answer to the integration question. |
| Lockheed / Northrop have legacy theater space presence; we're the new entrant | We bid commercial sensor data + commercial AI software, not the integrated theater architecture. Different procurement lane (DIU OTA vs. legacy program-of-record). |
| ARKA transition team isn't ready for outside customer pitches | Decision G precondition: transition-lead sign-off before any DIU material goes external. If sign-off can't happen in 30 days, the 30-day window slides — but the demand signal is active NOW so slip is real cost. |
| ARKA's pre-acquisition customers may not authorize CACI to use the names in pitches | First call with ARKA transition lead must surface what reference-customer naming is OK |

## What success looks like

- **30 days:** Larry Jordan meeting on calendar; ARKA transition-lead sign-off on pitch deck; preliminary readout from Emslie/Hannah on PWSA pull-through fit
