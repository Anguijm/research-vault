# Play cards

Per-play deep-dive cards. Each file is a focused one-to-two-page analysis of a single business-development play within this research track.

A play card is distinct from the research file at `../00_research-file.md`. The research file is the comprehensive analysis covering the whole opportunity. A play card focuses on one specific play — a single teaming-partner option, a single capability bid, a single contract-vehicle path — and goes deep on whatever makes that play succeed or fail.

The play-card template lives at `../../../_templates/play-card.md`.

Filename convention: `play-N-short-slug-YYYY-MM-DD.md`. The `N` is the play number from the corresponding section of the research file (so play 2 from §7.1 of PMTEC's research file becomes `play-2-...`). The short-slug is one or two words describing the play. The date is when the play card was first written.

If a play card is significantly revised (not just edited), create a new file with the new date rather than overwriting; old play cards can be informative as the play evolves.
