# Marine Log’s Ship Repair USA 2026

### Event Info

**Marine Log’s Ship Repair USA was created to address the unique challenges of small and medium-sized ship repair yards, along with the vessel owners and operators who rely on them.**

Ship Repair USA stands out by focusing exclusively on the needs of the ship repair and vessel operations sectors. Leveraging our extensive community of experts, our thoughtful conference sessions give attendees exclusive insights on the maintenance and retrofit processes. Real-world case studies provide actionable takeaways that attendees can apply to their operations, helping them stay ahead of trends and future-proof their businesses.

**For shipyards**, the event covers cost-effective repair project management, the integration of modern technologies, and staying compliant with the latest regulatory standards. Workforce development sessions will help shipyards build the skilled labor force necessary for long-term success.

**For vessel owners and operators**, Ship Repair USA offers affordable repair and maintenance solutions, risk management strategies, and networking opportunities with suppliers and shipyards. The event also highlights innovations that improve vessel performance, reduce downtime, and extend lifespans.

Join us in Jacksonville, FL, for the leading event for the leading event for ship repair, retrofitting, and maintenance professionals.
